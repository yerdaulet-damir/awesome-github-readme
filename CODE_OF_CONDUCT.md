# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of level of experience, gender identity, race, ethnicity, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**

- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Helping newcomers with their first contribution
- Giving credit where credit is due

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Spam or low-effort self-promotion
- Publishing others' private information

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public interaction related to this project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate.
