## Pet Streak Cards — Your Streak as an Evolving Animal

Your GitHub commit streak powers a pet that grows as you code. Miss a day and it resets. Keep going and it evolves through 9 stages.

> `seed (0d)` → `sprout (4d)` → `grind (10d)` → `streak (20d)` → `god (30d)` → `legend (50d)` → `mythic (100d)` → `eternal (200d)` → `transcended (365d)`
